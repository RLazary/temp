ABOUT US

At Skill Games of America, our mission is to lead the industry in crafting top-tier skill-based and bingo-based gaming software. We achieve this through a steadfast commitment to innovation, accountability, and delivering unparalleled customer satisfaction. 

Skill Games of America, LLC (SGA), headquartered in Ohio, is rapidly emerging as a leader in the game design and development industry. With over 20 years of experience in software development and route management for coin-operated machinery, our team combines expertise with innovation to deliver exceptional gaming solutions. 

At SGA, we specialize in creating versatile gaming software that can be deployed across multiple platforms, including Mac, Windows, and Linux. Our ever-expanding roster of games features captivating themes, stunning graphics, and immersive sound effects designed to keep players engaged and coming back for more.

From our visionary Creative Director to our skilled Programmers, our dedicated team is committed to developing high-quality products that set new industry standards.

Service excellence is at the heart of SGA. We ensure quick and seamless deployment, with systems that are user-friendly and easy to master. Our support team will have you up and running in just hours, making the transition smooth and hassle-free.

Contact us today to discover how Skill Games of America can elevate your gaming experience!
